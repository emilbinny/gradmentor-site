Guidance Built on Lived Experience

GradMentor was founded by someone who has been exactly where you are — and earned the admits to prove the approach works.

Emil Binny

Principal Founding Advisor, GradMentor

GradMentor was born from a simple observation: most applicants to elite MBA and PhD programmes are qualified — but very few know how to present that qualification compellingly. The difference between an admit and a ding is rarely about grades or test scores. It's about story, strategy, and positioning.

Emil brings first-hand knowledge of the admissions process from the applicant's side — having navigated top-tier MBA and PhD applications himself. His advisory work draws on research experience across multiple institutions, a deep understanding of how admissions committees think, and a network of advisors and alumni at programmes including Harvard, Wharton, Chicago Booth, and INSEAD.

GradMentor's approach is rigorous, personalised, and results-oriented — built around the client's unique story, not a templated process.

Our Philosophy

Guide. Strategize. Succeed.

The GradMentor tagline is also our methodology. Every engagement flows through these three principles.

Guide

Every client relationship begins with listening. We invest time in understanding your background, your goals, and what makes you genuinely distinctive before we advise on a single application element.

Strategize

School selection, positioning, narrative architecture, and application timing — everything is deliberate. We approach each application as a strategic product designed for a specific audience: the admissions committee.

Succeed

Success means the admit you want, from the programme that fits your goals — ideally with funding. We don't disengage after essay submission. We're with you through interview prep, waitlists, and final decisions.
